Our Mission Statement

The values and vision that drive everything we do.

At The Tech Patron LLC, we provide honest, practical IT and cybersecurity consultation to home users, small businesses, and nonprofits. We empower customers to take control of their technical environments through accessible education and realistic solutions. Our goal is to make security expertise available to everyone, regardless of budget or technical background. We help clients build lasting confidence in using technology safely and effectively. Ultimately, Tech Patron exists to create a more resilient, empowered community.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person has access to the technology expertise and support they need to succeed. We are committed to being the technology partner our clients can trust.

Our Core Values

Integrity We operate with transparency and honesty in every client relationship, always.
Excellence We hold ourselves to your standards in every service we deliver.
Innovation We stay at the forefront of technology so our clients have access to the best solutions.
Service Our clients' success is our success. We are here to serve, always.
Accountability We own our work and stand behind every commitment we make.

Our Commitment to You

We understand that technology challenges can feel overwhelming. That's why The Tech Patron is dedicated to making the complex simple. Utilizing clear communication, reliable support, and solutions that are genuinely necessary for your goals.

Whether you're an individual, non-profit, or just starting your business, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Why "The Tech Patron"?

A patron is someone who empowers the success of others. That's exactly what we strive to be for everyone we serve. Technology is our expertise; your success is the result.
